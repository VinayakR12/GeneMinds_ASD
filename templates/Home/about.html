About Our GeneMinds ASD

Empowering early detection, fostering awareness, and revolutionizing autism research through AI and compassion.

🌟 Why This Project Was Born

In India, awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is still limited. Many children go undiagnosed until later stages, missing critical opportunities for early intervention. Our platform bridges this gap by providing accessible, intelligent, and research-driven tools to support early detection and understanding of ASD.

🚀 Accessibility

Make ASD risk prediction and education available to every family and doctor, without barriers.

💡 Early Detection

Enable early screening to help children receive timely support and intervention programs.

🌈 Awareness Building

Spread scientific knowledge about ASD symptoms, challenges, and the power of acceptance.

✨ Key Features of Our Platform

🤖 Smart ASD Prediction

Use an AI-driven form to assess potential ASD risk quickly, based on carefully designed questions.

💬 Intelligent Chatbot

Ask questions about ASD and receive helpful, researched answers from our AI chatbot.

📚 Research Tools

Dive into genetic research and scientific data, specially curated to understand ASD causes and patterns.

📈 Data Insights

Explore real-world statistics and demographics related to ASD in India and globally.

🛠️ How It Works

Step 1: Take the Assessment

Fill out the prediction form with basic details and behavioral observations.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Our model analyzes inputs using trained Machine Learning algorithms based on real ASD research.

Step 3: Instant Result

Receive a risk estimation instantly, with recommendations for next steps if needed.

🔬 Our Research Behind the System

Our AI model was trained on validated ASD screening datasets and enhanced using genetic research findings. We focused particularly on patterns prevalent in Indian and global demographics to ensure accuracy. Our prediction form questions are carefully chosen based on medical and psychological research studies.

💎 What Makes Us Unique

🌐 India-Focused Research

Unlike global tools, our model focuses on India-specific ASD patterns and awareness gaps.

🎯 Multi-Utility Platform

Combines prediction, education, chatbot interaction, and research exploration — all in one ecosystem.
